Feature : **An Ze, the Warlock Kingdom**
-- * --
Prometheum was first discovered in An Ze in the early 2nd century, its uses kept secret for centuries by its artificers and witch-kings. Their magical power made them practically unassailable, from the Yeoxli Empire through to the modern era - only with the advent of heavy artillery did they finally fall, crushed beneath the overwhelming might of the Nexus. The witches of An Ze are extremely effective auxiliaries, when recruited - so too are their insurgents, and the chaos they are capable of.

The particular brand of magic used by An Ze would be considered excessively extravagant elsewhere. Blessed with a natural abundance of prometheum, their artificers had little issue with its expenditure as a magical fuel. Zeian magics are powered by the controlled detonation of prometheum, formed of an unblemished crystalline core and a thin, rune-forged metallic shell - these magical surges can be chained and amplified through careful geometric arrangement, providing dramatic empowerment to witch-spells or serving as potent battlefield weapons in their own right. The art is far from lost, but rarely practiced today; prometheum has far too many uses to be affordable for a Zeian-style thaumaturge in the wider world.

An Ze's mines supply a huge portion of the Nexus's prometheum, second only to imports from First Stars. They have been developed extensively since their capture by Eiyanixie, combining native Zeian artifice with Siayeo technology and sacred hydrothaumaturgy to excavate ever deeper - so intensive is their activity, that the limits of production are imposed by the throughput of its elevators and the risk of prometheum detonation. Even now they continue to expand - prometheum demand is always rising, and new sources are always sought.
