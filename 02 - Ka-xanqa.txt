*:: KHA-XANQA ::*

Pre-Siayeo automata consisted primarily of hydraulic puppetry, ensembles of repetitive engines directed by the governance of a punctured wheel. Though some examples of this technique exhibited great mechanical artistry, such as the legendary Laughing Labyrinth - warden and tormentor both to the prisoners interned within it - they were rigid, inflexible things, incapable of acting beyond the unchanging circumstances maintained about them. Their tales spread far and wide, however, guiding the hands of many a craftsman in the lands of Yeoxli and beyond - most .
